Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

When you create an account on nicespaceship.ai, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Display name (optional, for your profile)
  • Usage data (blueprints activated, missions run, XP earned)

If you connect Google Workspace (Gmail, Calendar, Drive), we access your data only through read-only OAuth scopes. NICE never stores your emails, calendar events, or files — agents process them in real-time and discard the data.

What We Don't Collect

  • We don't sell your data to third parties
  • We don't use your data for advertising
  • We don't store your Google Workspace content
  • We don't track you across other websites

LLM Provider Data

When you run missions, your prompts are sent to the selected LLM provider (Google, Anthropic, OpenAI, Mistral, DeepSeek, or xAI) through our server-side proxy. We do not store your prompts or responses beyond the current session. Each provider has their own data handling policies.

Payment Data

Token purchases are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card number. Stripe handles all payment security.

Cookies & Local Storage

NICE uses localStorage (not cookies) to store your preferences, theme selection, XP progress, and session data. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Data Hosting

Your account data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL database) hosted in the US West region. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings in the app. Email ben@nicespaceship.com if you need assistance.

Open Source

NICE is open source (MIT License). You can audit exactly what data we collect and how we handle it by reviewing the code at github.com/nicespaceship/nice.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email ben@nicespaceship.com.
