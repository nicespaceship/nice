01 · About

The Premier Indoor
Las Vegas Gun Range

American Shooters is a veteran-operated shooting range and gun store located just minutes from the Las Vegas Strip at Arville and Spring Mountain. We believe in serving those who serve America — with honor, integrity, and an unforgettable experience for every visitor.

Whether you want to fire a machine gun once or train weekly as a member, our experienced staff is ready to guide you through every step. Travelers, locals, first-timers, and seasoned marksmen are all welcome.
