About NICE SPACESHIP

Building the WordPress of AI agents. One blueprint at a time.

The Mission

AI is the most powerful technology since the internet, but most people can't use it beyond asking a chatbot questions.

NICE SPACESHIP is changing that. We're building a platform where anyone — business owners, teachers, parents, creators — can deploy AI agent workflows without coding, without API keys, and without a computer science degree.

The blueprint system turns complex AI orchestration into something as simple as browsing an app store: find what you need, activate it, and let the agents work.

The Approach

Open Source. The entire platform is MIT licensed. See the code, contribute blueprints, self-host if you want. Transparency builds trust.

Multi-LLM. We're not locked to one AI provider. NICE connects to Claude, Gemini, GPT-5, Mistral, DeepSeek, and Grok — routing each task to the best model automatically.

Business-First. We launch focused on small businesses because they have the clearest ROI: replace $1,300/month in tools with $29.99/month. Then we expand to serve everyone.
