The Mission

AI is the most powerful technology since the internet, but most people can't use it beyond asking a chatbot questions.

NICE SPACESHIP is changing that. We're building a platform where anyone — business owners, teachers, parents, creators — can deploy AI agent workflows without coding, without API keys, and without a computer science degree.

The Blueprint System

Spaceships

A Spaceship is your top-level orchestrator. It represents a complete business workflow — a restaurant, a marketing agency, a law firm. When you deploy a Spaceship, it automatically creates a crew of AI agents configured for that specific operation.

Each Spaceship has a crew capacity determined by its class. A Scout holds 1-2 agents. A Flagship holds 11+. As you earn XP and rank up, you unlock higher-class ships with more slots and rarer blueprints.

Agents

Agents are the workers. Each one has a role (analytics, content, marketing, support), a set of tools, and a system prompt that defines its behavior. Agents execute real tasks — drafting emails, analyzing data, scheduling posts, generating reports.

Every agent is powered by an AI model. NICE routes each task to the best available model automatically. Free users get Gemini Flash. Pro users unlock Claude, GPT-5, and 10+ models across text, image, video, and voice.

How They Work Together

You browse the catalog, pick a Spaceship blueprint, and activate it. The Spaceship deploys with a pre-configured crew. You can swap agents in and out of slots, connect integrations (Gmail, Calendar, Drive), and run missions — real tasks your agents execute.

Rarity & Progression

Blueprints come in five rarities: Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, and Mythic. Higher rarities unlock as you rank up from Ensign to Fleet Admiral. Mythic blueprints can never be purchased — they must be earned through milestones.
