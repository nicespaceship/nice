The Mission

AI is the most powerful technology since the internet, but most people can't use it beyond chatbots.

NICE SPACESHIP makes AI agents accessible to everyone — no coding, no API keys. Browse a blueprint, activate it, and let the agents work.

The Blueprint System

Every workflow starts with a blueprint. Browse, activate, deploy.

Spaceships

A Spaceship is your orchestrator — a complete business workflow with a pre-configured crew of AI agents. Deploy one and your team is ready to work.

Rarity & Progression

Five rarities from Common to Mythic. Rank up to unlock rarer blueprints. Mythic can never be purchased — only earned.

Agents

Agents are the workers — each with a role, tools, and an AI model. They draft emails, analyze data, schedule posts, and generate reports.

How They Work Together

Pick a Spaceship, activate it, connect your integrations, and run missions. Swap agents in and out as needed.
