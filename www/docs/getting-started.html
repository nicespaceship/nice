Getting Started

Go from zero to a working AI agent team in under 5 minutes.

1. Sign Up

Go to nicespaceship.ai and click Sign In. You can sign up with email or Google. No credit card required.

Every new account gets 100K free tokens and access to Gemini Flash (unlimited, free forever).

2. Browse Blueprints

Click Bridge in the sidebar to open the blueprint catalog. You'll see two tabs:

  • Spaceships — complete business workflows with pre-configured agent crews
  • Agents — individual AI workers with specific roles and tools

Start with a Spaceship. Pick one that matches your business (marketing agency, restaurant, law firm, etc.) and click Activate.

3. Deploy Your Spaceship

When you activate a Spaceship, it automatically deploys with a crew of agents configured for that workflow. Each agent has:

  • A role (analytics, content, marketing, support)
  • A set of tools (summarize, web search, email, calendar)
  • An AI model assigned automatically by NICE

Your crew appears in the Schematic view — a visual map of your spaceship and its agents.

4. Run a Mission

Open the Prompt Panel (bottom of screen or Cmd+K) and type a task. For example:

  • "Draft a welcome email for new customers"
  • "Analyze our Q1 sales data"
  • "Create a social media content calendar for this week"

NICE routes your request to the best agent on your crew, picks the optimal AI model, and executes the task. Results appear in the Missions tab.

5. Connect Integrations

For full power, connect your Google Workspace:

  1. Go to Security > Integrations
  2. Click Sign in with Google
  3. Authorize Gmail, Calendar, and Drive access

Once connected, your agents can read emails, check your calendar, and search your Drive — all through natural language commands.
