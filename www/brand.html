Lockup · two contrasts only

Monochrome by rule.

The SPACESHIP lockup carries the brand at full strength. Render it in one of two forms: black on white, or white on black. Glyph and wordmark always share a single color. Any other treatment dilutes the mark.

Palette · pure monochrome

Black, white, gray. That's it.

NICE uses monochrome surfaces by rule. No brand color appears on any surface, text, badge, or border. The single chromatic moment lives on the CTA (Sapphire #0F52BA). Restraint defines the brand; color drives the action.

Brand standards

The rules in one place.

Use this section as the authoritative reference when applying the NICE brand. Every choice on this page maps to a rule below.

  • Color discipline. Surfaces, text, badges, and borders use pure monochrome: black, white, and gray only. Sapphire #0F52BA owns the single place the brand color appears.
  • The lockup. Black-on-white or white-on-black. Glyph and wordmark always share a single color. These are the only valid forms.
  • Surfaces (light). #ffffff primary · #fafafa section alt · #f5f5f5 hover and input.
  • Surfaces (dark). #0a0a0a primary · #141414 section alt · #1f1f1f hover and input.
  • Borders. Hairline only. Light: #e5e5e5 / #d4d4d4. Dark: #262626 / #404040.
  • Text. Light: #0a0a0a body, #525252 muted, #a3a3a3 dim. Dark: #fafafa body, #a3a3a3 muted, #737373 dim.
  • CTA. Sapphire #0F52BA. Hover #1862ce. Ink #ffffff. Same value in both themes.
  • Buttons. Flat. No shadow, no glow, no gradient. Solid Sapphire (primary), white with hairline border (secondary), bg-on-hover only (ghost).
  • Radius scale. Badges / chips 4px · Buttons 6px · Content cards 12px · Blueprint cards 14px · Marketing frames + lockup tiles 12px. No pill shapes anywhere.
  • Typography. Inter for all UI and prose. Orbitron Black for the SPACESHIP wordmark only. JetBrains Mono for telemetry, hex values, and code.
  • Page rhythm. Pattern B sets the default for every page on nicespaceship.com. Pattern A's black interlude marks moments where the lockup carries full presence: About page, footer band, brand page.
  • Blueprint cards. Cards run their own visual system: pure black #000 stock plus the rarity and category palette in both themes. This applies only to card surfaces. Buttons and CTAs inside a card follow the brand rules.

Theme system

Light default. Dark supported.

NICE ships in two modes. Light is the default across nicespaceship.com and the app. Dark is a first-class supported mode, available alongside the named in-app skins. Both modes use a single body[data-theme] attribute that swaps every surface, text, and border token in one declaration.

Flips with the theme:

  • Page background, section alt, card surface, hover surface
  • Text (body, muted, dim)
  • Borders (default and prominent)
  • Nav glyph and dashboard sidebar glyph (via currentColor, inheriting from --text-strong)

Constant across both modes:

  • Sapphire CTA. #0F52BA carries the brand identity across both modes.
  • Lockup contrasts. Black-on-white and white-on-black both stand as canonical forms.
  • Pattern A's black interlude. The lockup-showcase band always uses pure black.
  • Blueprint cards. Pure black card stock plus the rarity palette, in both modes.
