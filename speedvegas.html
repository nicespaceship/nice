03 · Your Experience

The Full Journey

From the moment you arrive to the moment you relive your session on video — every touchpoint is orchestrated. Our agents handle the operations so every guest stays in the moment.

STEP::01
Arrival & Check-In
Arrive at SpeedVegas. The booking agent confirms your reservation, validates walk-ins, and logs arrival time. No queues — your profile is ready.
STEP::02
Registration & Waiver
Digital waiver signing, driver's license scan, and wristband issuance — all handled and stored automatically. Your USB video key is linked to your profile.
STEP::03
Technical Briefing
Professional safety and driving technique briefing. The guest journey agent schedules your slot automatically and alerts staff when you're ready to advance.
STEP::04
Discovery Laps
Two guided laps in a Porsche Macan S with your instructor. Completion is tracked and triggers automatic progression to pit lane assignment.
STEP::05
Pit Lane Assignment
The pit lane ops agent automatically matches you with your car and instructor. The digital pit board updates in real-time — no manual coordination needed.
STEP::06
Helmet & Drive
Fitted for your helmet, then released to your designated supercar. Your USB key links to the car's onboard recording system. Every lap, captured.
STEP::07
Lap Timing & Data
RaceFacer records every lap in real-time. The lap time intelligence agent compiles your performance data and prepares your personalized session report.
STEP::08
Checkout & Video
Return your USB key and helmet. Purchase your in-car video, photos, and add-ons via POS. Your performance report is emailed automatically within minutes.
